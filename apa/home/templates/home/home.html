Update Proctology - 2017

Malavan

A national conference with live demonstration of surgeries was conducted at Malavan, Maharashtra. More than 600 delegates from all over India were benefited by live demonstration of 65 surgeries in 3 days followed by feast of lectures of well-known faculties. Sightseeing & water sports were arranged by APA for delegates... Conference was blessed by the presence of Shri.Shripadji Naik, Minister of AYUSH.

Update Proctology - 2019

Ghataprabha

Second national conference with live demonstration of surgeries was held at Ghataprabha, Karnataka. Motto of this conference to enrol max no. of UG PG students & encourage them to choose the field of Proctology. More than 800 delegates registered for the conference.

Proctology Lecture Series - 2020

Mumbai

During the lockdown period APA organised 7 days lecture series in association with Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik. Basic concepts in Proctology along with some advance treatments all topics covered by eminent speakers... all topics covered by eminent speakers.
